Madagascar Travel & Nature Guide Website Plan

Site Name

Madagascar Travel & Nature Guide

This name was chosen because it clearly describes the purpose of the website. It focuses on Madagascar's natural attractions, wildlife, culture, and travel information while helping visitors easily understand what the site offers.

Optional Domain: madagascartravelguide.org

Site Purpose

The website provides useful information about Madagascar, including its geography, wildlife, culture, tourist destinations, and weather conditions. Visitors will be able to explore popular attractions, learn interesting facts, view images, and access travel-related information through a clean and responsive design.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in Madagascar for nature and wildlife lovers?
  • What is the weather like in Madagascar before planning a trip?
  • What unique animals can only be found in Madagascar?
  • What cultural traditions should tourists know before visiting Madagascar?

Color Schema

Forest Green (#2E8B57)

Used for headings, navigation bars, buttons, and accents. Represents Madagascar's rich forests and natural environment.

Sand Beige (#F4EBD0)

Used for page backgrounds and content sections to create a warm, natural appearance.

Typography

Poppins

Used for headings and navigation elements.

Open Sans

Used for body text and general content because it is highly readable.
