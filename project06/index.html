Madagascar is one of the world's most unique destinations, famous for its incredible biodiversity, stunning landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

About Madagascar

Madagascar is the fourth largest island in the world and is located in the Indian Ocean off the southeastern coast of Africa. The island is home to thousands of species found nowhere else on Earth, including lemurs, chameleons, and baobab trees.
